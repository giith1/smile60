About Smile60

Smile60, a UK-based company established in 2012, specializes in professional teeth whitening services. With over 30 years of experience in the dental industry, its founders have observed the progression of teeth whitening techniques. They have analyzed the pros and cons of various methods, including home kits, bleaching trays, UV lights, and laser devices. Leveraging their research and expertise, they have designed a proprietary laser teeth whitening system that is safe, efficient, and highly effective.

How It Works

Smile60 employs cutting-edge laser technology known as Beyond+ to achieve teeth whitening of up to 12 shades in just one hour. This advanced system is non-invasive, pain-free, and gentle on enamel and gums. A certified technician oversees the procedure, beginning with the application of a whitening gel to the teeth.

This gel, rich in hydrogen peroxide, penetrates the tooth surface to effectively break down stains and discoloration. The laser light then activates the gel, speeding up the chemical process and enhancing the whitening effect. Throughout the procedure, the technician carefully monitors and adjusts the process to ensure optimal results.
