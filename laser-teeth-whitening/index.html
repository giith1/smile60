How Smile60’s Laser Teeth Whitening Work

Our laser teeth whitening procedure follows a structured, step-by-step process:

  • Consultation: During your initial visit, our specialists will assess your teeth and overall oral health to confirm your suitability for laser teeth whitening.
  • Preparation: We clean your teeth thoroughly and apply a protective barrier to safeguard your gums during the treatment.
  • Whitening Gel Application: A specially formulated whitening gel is carefully applied to your teeth.
  • Laser Activation: The gel is activated using a laser, which targets and breaks down stains and discoloration for effective whitening.
  • Rinse and Reveal: After the procedure, we rinse your teeth, revealing an instantly whiter, brighter smile you’ll love.

Official FAQs
What is Smile60 Instant Laser Teeth Whitening?

Smile60 Instant Laser Teeth Whitening is a professional teeth whitening treatment that uses advanced laser technology to quickly and effectively whiten your teeth. It is designed to deliver noticeable results in just one session, making it an ideal solution for those looking to enhance their smile instantly.

How long does the Smile60 whitening procedure take?

The entire Smile60 procedure typically takes about 60 minutes. This includes preparation, application of the whitening gel, and laser activation. You can achieve visibly whiter teeth in just a single session.

Is Smile60 Instant Laser Teeth Whitening safe?

Yes, Smile60 uses safe and proven laser technology in combination with a specially formulated whitening gel. The treatment is carried out by trained professionals to ensure minimal sensitivity and maximum safety for your teeth and gums.

How much whiter can my teeth get with Smile60?

Results vary depending on individual factors, such as the level of discoloration and enamel condition. On average, most clients see an improvement of up to 6-12 shades whiter after a single Smile60 treatment.

How long do the results of Smile60 Laser Teeth Whitening last?

The results can last anywhere from 6 months to 2 years, depending on your lifestyle habits. To maintain your brighter smile, avoid staining foods and beverages, practice good oral hygiene, and consider touch-up treatments as needed.
